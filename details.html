About This Event

A traditional winter rice cake festival celebrating Bengali heritage under the cool winter breeze. The event features various stalls showcasing authentic handmade pithas. A major highlight is the competition where the top three stalls will be awarded prizes for their presentation and taste.

Objectives

  • Celebrate and preserve traditional Bengali culinary heritage
  • Encourage student entrepreneurship through stall management
  • Recognize creativity and skill with competitive awards
  • Strengthen the community bond through seasonal festivities
